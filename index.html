Why Computer Science?

Computer science is an incredibly important topic, especially in today’s rapidly growing technological world. This new, advanced age will only continue growing as consumers continue demanding more from corporate entities that are trying to have the best and newest technology in record times. With this promptly growing business, it is important to understand what it is about and how it works. It is more important than ever to be an informed consumer when buying these new technologies and understanding the risks and rewards from them. Most people do not understand how their data is being sold and taken advantage of by third party companies or how easy it is to break into a wireless and unprotected network. Not only that, but many companies are turning more tech-savy daily, so as someone who is in a job position or looking for one, it is important to keep up with programs and applications your job might need.
